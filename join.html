PhD Students

We are actively recruiting PhD students interested in computational cognitive science, human-AI interaction, and AI alignment. Please apply through:

  • UW CSE PhD Program
  • Foster School PhD Program

Interested students should mention Max Kleiman-Weiner in their application to ensure that we will review it.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome inquiries from potential postdoctoral researchers with strong backgrounds in computational cognitive science, machine learning, or related fields. Please contact us with your CV and research interests.

UW Undergraduate and Master's Students

UW undergraduate and master's students interested in research opportunities should email their resume and a brief description of their research interests. Students should be prepared to commit to at least a year working 10-20 hours per week during the academic year and full-time during the summer.
